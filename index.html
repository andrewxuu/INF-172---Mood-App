How we do it

The Problem

Students face immense academic stress and often turn to music for relief. However, major streaming platforms rely on generalized playlists that fail to capture nuanced, individual emotional responses.

Our Solution

Our AI chatbot provides personalized music recommendations based on self-reported emotions. By analyzing your existing playlists and real-time interactions, the system adapts to your unique tastes without relying on invasive biometric tracking.

Expected Impact

By delivering immediate, tailored music recommendations, Mood Bot mitigates burnout and transforms everyday listening into an accessible, dynamic mental health resource.

Tracking Our Progress

Project Milestones

A clear, strategic roadmap ensuring we build a robust and accurate recommendation engine.

Phase 1: Project Setup

Analyze current AI-driven music platforms to identify limitations. Establish core project architecture and deploy the preliminary project website.

Phase 2: Gap Analysis

Pinpoint critical gaps in existing market solutions, specifically addressing the lack of personalization and over-reliance on inaccurate biometric tracking.

Phase 3: AI Training

Develop and train adaptive learning algorithms using continuous user input and playlist data to accurately map musical tastes to emotional states.

Phase 4: Final Delivery

Launch a highly accurate, accessible, and user-friendly AI chatbot system that delivers real-time music recommendations.
